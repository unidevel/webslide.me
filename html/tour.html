What is webslide.me?

webslide.me is a free service to create webslides, a selfmade format of presentations. Webslides were created for the web; and there is no additional software or plugin required to use them within your web browser.

webslide.me also offer the capabilities to create, play, share or (remote-)control your webslides. Have a look at the detailed feature descriptions below in this Tour of webslide.me.

Accessibility included
The webslide.me HTML5 and CSS3 presentation framework makes usage of ARIA-related technologies to provide compatibility to screenreaders. It's the first presentation software that is able to provide accessibility to visual impared people.

Features

Introduction

This introduction shows the usage of the webslide.me editor.

Create webslides

The editor allows you to create beautiful webslides within minutes.

There are several predefined color schemes, animations and styles to simplify the usage of the webslide editor as much as possible.

The web browser allows you to use images, videos and other media or content that you find on the web.
So, it's up to you which content you may include in your presentations.

Both the editor and player are simple to learn.
No technical knowledge required. Promised.

Share webslides

Share your webslides on Twitter, Facebook and among other social networks using easy-to-remember links.

(Remote-) Control your webslides

Imagine you've got a webinar or an international conference with others and you want to distribute the slides for your presentation. Here's what you can do:

  1. Share your Play-Link of the webslide to others
  2. Login and visit the Control
  3. Start controlling your presentation
  4. Any other viewer, no matter where he is or what web browser he is using, will now see the same slide and the same animation step you are talking about.
